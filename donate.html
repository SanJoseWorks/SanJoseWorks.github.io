OUR FOCUS

San Jose Works is committed in engaging, connecting, and preparing young adults, where they are able to build a strong foundation for their future careers. We want to guide our youth in becoming contributing members of the workforce and our communities. Our four pillars of “Work-Readiness” provide youth the structure needed in today’s workforce.

Career Mentorship

Financial Literacy

Career Networking

Sector-Based Skill Growth
